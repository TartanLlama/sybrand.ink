Sy Brand

Bio

Sy is a queer non-binary poet and filmmaker living in Edinburgh, Scotland.

They write through the haze of cat-/child-induced sleep deprivation to try and make sense of gender, relationships, and ADHD.

You can find them on Twitter @TartanLlama.
